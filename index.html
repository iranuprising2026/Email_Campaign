Email Dutch Politicians

Action Steps:

🚩 You can check the Live Action Tracker to choose politicians with lower numbers.

Steps:
1. Choose Campaign Topic (Sit-in or Executions).
2. Enter Name/City.
3. Select Politician & Email Version.
4. Click Generate Content.
5. Click Mobile or Web.
⚠️ Mobile: Use Gmail (Outlook Mobile has bugs).

ℹ️ You can see the English translation of the Dutch email in the preview box below.
